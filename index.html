About Our Farm

Sunshine Poultry Farm was established in 2010 with a mission to provide the highest quality poultry products while maintaining sustainable and ethical farming practices.

We believe in raising our birds in a stress-free, natural environment, allowing them to roam freely and enjoy natural sunshine. This approach results in healthier birds and better-quality products for our customers.

Our Mission

To produce the highest quality poultry products while promoting sustainable farming practices and animal welfare.

Our Vision

To become the leading sustainable poultry farm in the region, known for our quality, integrity, and commitment to the environment.

Why Choose Us

Sustainable Practices

We implement eco-friendly farming methods to minimize environmental impact and promote sustainability.

Animal Welfare

Our birds are raised in spacious, clean environments with access to outdoor areas and natural sunlight.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes strict quality checks to ensure the highest standards of freshness and nutrition.
